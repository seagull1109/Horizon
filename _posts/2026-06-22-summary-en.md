## [OpenMontage: Open-Source AI Video Production](https://github.com/calesthio/OpenMontage) ⭐️ 8.0/10

OpenMontage has been released as the world's first open-source agentic video production system, featuring 12 pipelines, 52 tools, and 500+ agent skills that transform AI coding assistants into complete video production studios. This development democratizes professional video production by making advanced AI-powered tools accessible to creators, potentially revolutionizing content creation workflows and reducing the barrier to entry for high-quality video production. The system can process content from YouTube, Shorts, Reels, TikTok, or local clips, transforming them into structured production plans following the same process as real production teams, but automated by AI agents.

**Background**: Agentic video production systems represent a new frontier in AI content creation, where AI agents can perform complex creative tasks traditionally requiring human expertise. The concept of 'agent skills' refers to specialized capabilities that extend AI functionality with specific knowledge and workflows. Open-source initiatives in AI video production are increasingly important as they allow transparency, customization, and community-driven innovation in creative technologies.
